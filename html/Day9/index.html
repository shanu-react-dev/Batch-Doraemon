Dhoni is a Good Captain in the entire world

Mahendra Singh Dhoni (born July 7, 1981), popularly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and one of the greatest captains in the history of the sport. He led India to victory in the ICC T20 World Cup, the ICC Cricket World Cup, and the ICC Champions Trophy, becoming the only captain to win all three major ICC trophies. Known for his calm demeanor and sharp finishing skills, Dhoni was a powerful wicketkeeper-batsman. He played for Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League, leading them to multiple titles. His leadership, humility, and match-winning performances have made him a legendary figure in world cricket.
